Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Uniqva Reels ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use this Site or buy our product, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site, you agree to this policy.

We keep it simple: we only collect what we need to process your order and give you access. We never see or store your card number, UPI PIN, or bank password — those are handled entirely by Cashfree.

1. Information we collect

a) Information you give us

  • Name — for your order and receipt.
  • Email address — to send confirmation/receipt and support.
  • Phone number — used at checkout (e.g. for UPI) and support.

b) Payment information

Payments are processed by Cashfree. Your payment details (card, UPI, netbanking) are entered directly with Cashfree and are governed by Cashfree's own privacy policy. We only receive limited confirmation data such as a payment ID and status.

c) Automatically collected

Like most websites, basic technical data (such as your browser type or general usage) may be logged by our hosting provider for security and reliability. If we run ad campaigns, our ad platforms may set cookies to measure performance.

2. How we use your information

  • To process your payment and deliver access to the product.
  • To send you your receipt and important information about your purchase.
  • To provide customer support (for example, re-sharing your access link).
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud or technical issues.
  • To measure and improve our marketing, where applicable.

3. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us run the business:

  • Cashfree — payment processing.
  • Netlify — website hosting and delivery.
  • Google Drive — hosting and delivery of the digital files.
  • Advertising/analytics platforms (such as Meta), only if and where we run campaigns.

We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Cookies

The Site itself uses minimal cookies needed to function. Third-party tools (payment, ads, analytics) may set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data retention

We keep order-related information (such as name, email, phone, and payment ID) only as long as needed to provide support, keep records, and meet legal or accounting requirements. After that, it is deleted or anonymised.

6. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Payment security is handled by Cashfree using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

You may contact us to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to our legal record-keeping needs.

To make a request, email us using the details below.

8. Children

This Site and product are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.
