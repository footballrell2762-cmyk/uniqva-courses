Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to the digital product sold by Uniqva Reels ("we", "us", "our") on this Site. By completing a purchase, you agree to this policy.

In short: Our product is a digital download delivered instantly. Because access is given right after payment, purchases are generally non-refundable. However, if you were charged but did not receive access, or you were charged twice, we will make it right — see below.

1. Nature of the product

You are buying a digital product — a bundle of 2000+ football video clips delivered through a Google Drive folder. The full content is unlocked and made available to you immediately after a successful, verified payment. There is no physical shipment.

2. Cancellation

As delivery is instant and automatic, an order cannot be cancelled once payment is completed and access has been delivered. If you change your mind before completing payment, simply do not complete the checkout — no charge will be made.

3. Refunds — general rule

Because the product is digital and access is granted immediately, all sales are final and non-refundable once the access page/folder link has been delivered to you. This is standard for instantly-delivered digital goods, since the content cannot be "returned" after it has been accessed or downloaded.

4. When we will refund

We will issue a full refund in the following genuine cases:

  • Payment deducted, no access: Your payment was successfully charged but, due to a technical error on our side, you did not receive the access page or the folder link.
  • Duplicate payment: You were accidentally charged more than once for the same order. We will refund the extra amount(s).
  • Folder unavailable: The delivered Google Drive folder is genuinely inaccessible from our end and we are unable to restore your access within a reasonable time.

5. What is not eligible

  • Change of mind after access has been delivered.
  • "I already had similar clips" or "I didn't get the growth I expected" — the product is a resource; results are not guaranteed.
  • A third-party platform (Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, etc.) not approving, monetising, or allowing a clip you posted.
  • Issues caused by your own device, storage, or internet connection.

6. How to request a refund

If your situation falls under Section 4, contact us within 7 days of your payment with:

  • Your payment ID (from Cashfree / your bank message), and
  • The email and phone number used at checkout, and
  • A short description of the problem.

Email footballrell2762@gmail.com or message us on WhatsApp.

Once a refund is approved, it will be initiated through Cashfree to your original payment method. It typically takes 5–7 business days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
